Hard Conversations

A safe place to rehearse the ethics & compliance conversations most people never get to practice until they're in one for real. Pick a scenario, work through it, and get feedback measured against real law and standards — not opinion.

How it works

Each scenario is a short, branching conversation. The other person pushes back and adapts to your choices. At every decision you choose what to actually say. Afterward you're scored across four skills, each tied to a cited authority (FCPA, UK Bribery Act, Sarbanes-Oxley, Title VII, GDPR, ISO 37001, and more), with a model answer to compare against.

Nothing is tracked, stored, or sent anywhere — everything runs in your browser. Scenarios are fictional; feedback references real, publicly available standards and is for training, not legal advice.
